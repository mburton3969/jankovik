Company History

about our company

Our company was established in 1998 to give high-quality natural cosmetics to those customers who were looking for something more than basic makeup. We have gathered a friendly team of makeup and cosmetics enthusiasts and experts over the years. They helped us in the beginning and they are a vital part of our company even today. However, we aim to satisfy our customers not only with high-quality products but also with utmost services, and that’s why we still are No. 1 natural cosmetics supplier in the area. Here is a short look at our history and company development.

1995-2008

We started our company with the idea of creating natural beauty products available to everybody. We started with soaps, and the first one was Banana Moon in its cylinder shape, molded from unused drainpipes.This product became a bestseller months later.

In 1998 we have finally developed a concept of Natural Cosmetics, which we still use at our every store. This was the moment when we finally gained some recognition not only in California but also in other states. That’s how our professional ethics was born.

From 2001 to 2008, we have achieved great success and were recognized by American and Canadian customers. Our team was awarded “The Best Beauty Store” Award in 2007, and we also opened new stores in Seattle, Ontario, and New York. In 2008, we updated our website and store.

2009

Advertising campaign

In 2009, we put up a huge advertising campaign devoted to a variety of handmade products and our new product lines.

As handmade cosmetics is often unique for every client, we needed to give special attention to detail our customers preffered in our products. That’s why we decided to partner with an experienced agency, Promote2K.

2010-2015

In 2010 we began to offer our soaps and other skin care products in Europe. it was a huge achievement for us and we needed to adapt to European market in order to make it happen. All our products are still handmade; when we expand we employ more people not machines. We also stay true to our goals and we’re more like small stores.
