Our advertisements do not include unreasonable promises

Over the years, Natural Cosmetics have created handmade natural cosmetics, by the principles of Corneotherapy, for professional use and has received recognition from beauticians of beauty salons and SPA centers. In the compositions of our Skin Care products the modern knowledge about the structure and functions of the skin is combined with the latest technologies.

They have proven their effectiveness during centuries of experience. Under the new strategy of the company, cosmetics earlier supplied to salons and entirely new products are now proposed for home use according to salons techniques. Our skin care products are different from the “mass” cosmetics, by offering true naturalness.
