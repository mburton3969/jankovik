Company overview

about our company

Our products

The products available at our store are 100 % organic and healthy for you.

Savings

Our beauty and skin care products can save you a lot of money

Realism

Our advertisements do not include unreasonable promises

Natural Cosmetics is the embodiment of the vision of John and Jane Smiths, who founded the company in 1995 in Los angeles, CA, with one soap series. Early on, they saw the connection between wellness and beauty, plant fragrances and the potential for a profitable business that was also committed to social and environmental responsibility. At the heart of our company is its environmental mission—and a desire to set a business standard in environmental sustainability.

Our mission at Natural Cosmetics is to care for the world we live in, from the products we make to the ways in which we give back to society. We strive to set an example for environmental leadership and responsibility, not just in the world of beauty, but around the world. Our company ensures integrity and quality by using organically grown flowers and plants wherever possible and building relationships with indigenous people who are in alignment with our philosophy.

Change your lifestyle

For the hygiene of your body, we developed ecological and economic vegan soaps made from natural ingredients such as essential oils and plant oils.

Purchasing our 100% natural soaps is a first step towards a healthier lifestyle
