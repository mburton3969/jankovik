Join our team

become a part of our company

Working with us is always an adventure. Whether you’re a part-time sales ambassador, a manager on our manufacturing team or a financial analyst, the culture of our company flows through every area of the business. We work hard because we believe in the beauty and effectiveness of our fresh handmade products, and we’re thrilled to be helping people, animals and the planet with everything we do. here’s what you get while working with us:

  • Competitive starting salary with periodic reviews
  • 401(k) retirement savings plan
  • Comprehensive health and accident insurance
  • Group dental insurance
  • Group life insurance
  • Child-care tax advantage accounts
  • Healthcare tax advantage accounts

What we expect

Sales Manager

As our Sales Manager, you must be passionate about our products, ethics and delivering the ultimate customer service.If you are an individual who believes in team work, possesses organizational and time management skills, then we want you to join our team!

In-Store Manager

As an In-Store Manager, it is your responsibility to embrace our corporate culture and to lead, inspire and motivate your team to act as guardians of the brand.We are looking for an experienced manager who is enthusiastic about working on the shop floor and managing our online store.

Assistant Manager

We are looking for team member, who is passionate about handmade cosmetics and working with people and for people.As an Assistant manager at Natural Cosmetics, you will interact constantly with staff and customers through product demos and consultations.
