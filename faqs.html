faq

some of your questions

Q.

Do you have multiple locations?

A.

We have several locations in all big cities but you can also order directly from our website or call into the store to place a phone order.

Q.

How are your products different from others?

A.

Our products are just as much skin care products as they are skin care products. Our skin care is infused with the use of herbs and essential oils to ensure better healing.

Q.

If I don't live in the United States can I still order online?

A.

Yes! We offer shipping worldwide. Shipping prices will vary depending on country.

Q.

What order should I use for all my products?

A.

Facial soap, facial scrub, mask, toner, serum, moisturizer.

Q.

How often should I use a facial mask?

A.

We recommend 1-3 times a week.

Q.

What makes your lipsticks different?

A.

Our lipsticks are natural and infused with essential oils to provide anti-aging benefits.

Q.

Why are your facial soaps in a bar not a liquid?

A.

Liquid facial soap can be too harsh and less effective because the liquids benefits are penetrating into the hands not the face.Our facial soaps are a concentrated small bar so you can apply the soap directly to the face for more effective cleaning.

Q.

How do you use the cupcake and cake soaps?

A.

For cupcake soaps you can just rub it in your hands like a normal soap or you can use it with a wash cloth.For cake soaps you want to take a knife and cut your soap in half so that there is both frosting and cake bottom on each section.
